Project: DESITASTE

The project involves developing an innovative moblie application designed to helip users discover recipes based on the ingredients they already have at home. By focusing on ingredient-driven searches, the app aims to reduce food wastes, simplify meal planning and make cooking accessible for users with varying skill levels.

Features

  • Ingredient-Based Recipe Suggestions: Users input the ingredients they have, and the app provides tailored recipe options.
  • Search Filters: Users can filter recipes by dietary preferences,cuisine type,cooking time or difficulty level.
  • Dynamic Content Updates: Recipes are updated regularly to reflect seasonal ingredients, trending cuisines and user-generated feedback
  • User Interaction: Users can rate recipes, save favorites,and share their feedback to continuously refine the app's content.

Goals

  • Simplify meal preparation and decision-making for users.
  • Promote sustainability by reducing food waste through creative recipe ideas.
  • Create an intuitive and user-friendly platform for both novice and experienced cooks
